## Table

Very picky about table format. There must be the right amount of pipes, 3 consecutive hyphens per column of the separator line:

| a | b |
|---|---|
| c | d |

No outer pipes:

 a | b
---|---
 c | d

4 hyphens:

| a | b |
|----|---|
| c | d |

Space 3 hyphens:

| a | b |
| ---|---|
| c | d |

3 hyphens space:

| a | b |
|--- |---|
| c | d |

hyphen space 2 hyphens:

| a | b |
|- --|---|
| c | d |

2 hyphens:

| a | b |
|--|---|
| c | d |

no pipes:

| a | b |
|-------|
| c | d |
